## Overview

I2C (Inter-Integrated Circuit) is a synchronous, multi-master, multi-slave, packet-switched, single-ended,
serial communication bus. In simpler terms, it's a way to connect multiple devices (sensors, displays, etc.)
to your microcontroller using just two wires.
